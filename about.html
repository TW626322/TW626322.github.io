About Me

My name is Tyler A. Wilson, and I am a programmer and nerd at heart. I was born and raised in Ellisville my entire life. I graduated from South Jones High School in 2015 and begin at JCJC the following semester. Originally looking to go to Mississippi State for Computer Engineering, I decided after my first year that I liked programming more. In May, 2018, I graduated with an Associate of Applied Science degree in Information Systems Technology with an emphasis on programming.

Since then I have acquired two certifications that I am most proud of: a CIW Site Development Associate, and an MTA Software Development Fundamentals. I continue to seek out other certifications that would be valuable in my search for a career.

Design

Being able to design websites is something that I am proud to be able to do. Working as part of a team to build a creative and functional site is where I excel.

Program

Programming in general is where I believe that I have skills and an aptitude that sets me apart from my peers. I tend to code most things from scratch as that gives me more freedom to create.
